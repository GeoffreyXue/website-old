Remember to do css in order:

1. position, z-index, top/bottom/left/right, float
2. display, opacity, transform
3. overflow, clip
4. animation, transition
5. margin, border, width, height, padding
6. background, cursor
7. font-size, font-family, font-weight, font-style, text-align, color
8. :hover, :focus, :active, :before, :after,
